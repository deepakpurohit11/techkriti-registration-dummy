About the visit

Techkriti is a student-run technical and cultural festival at IIT Kanpur. The registration below is for external students interested in literature and cultural sessions. Please carry your student ID/government ID to the campus gate.

  • Dates: Tentative March
  • Venue: IIT Kanpur Campus
  • Organizer: Committee Techkriti IITK
    • 1. Increasing participation of women in literature programs

    IIT Kanpur aims to encourage and improve the participation of female students studying literature, especially from regions where representation has historically been low, such as the Northeastern states, Bihar, Jharkhand, and other underrepresented areas.

    2. Eligibility & Registration

    To be considered, students must fill out the registration form with accurate details. The selection will be based on the information submitted.

    3. Sponsored Visit for Selected Participants

    Students who are selected will receive a fully sponsored 4-day visit to IIT Kanpur, including travel support, accommodation, and food. Further communication will be done via email by an official organizer or committee member from IIT Kanpur.
