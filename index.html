Techkriti 2026 — Registration

Open registration for literature & arts students visiting IIT Kanpur. Please fill the form to join select Techkriti sessions. If selected, the travel and stay will be sponsored by the respective committees.

About the visit

  • Dates: Tentative March
  • Venue: IIT Kanpur Campus
  • Coordination Team: IIT Kanpur Techriti Team

Please carry your student ID/government ID to the campus gate.

The campus is taking focused steps to increase the participation of girl students with a literature background, especially from the Northeast, Bihar, Jharkhand and West Bengal, where representation has traditionally been low. Selected participants will receive full support, including a sponsored trip, accommodation and food.

Organized by the Techkriti Organizing Committee.
